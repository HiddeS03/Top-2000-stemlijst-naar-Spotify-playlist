How to use:

  1. Go to the NPO Radio 2 Top 2000 voting site and create your list
  2. Copy the submission URL (e.g., https://npo.nl/luister/stem/.../inzending/...)
  3. Paste the URL above and click "Fetch Songs"
  4. Click "Connect to Spotify" to authorize the app
  5. Your playlist will be created and opened in Spotify!

Note: You need to configure your Spotify Client ID in the code for this to work. Visit the Spotify Developer Dashboard to create an app and get your Client ID.
